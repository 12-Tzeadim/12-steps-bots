Step 9 - Words, Actions and Change of Attitude

What does this bot do?

This is an AI-based spiritual guide designed to accompany us in working on Step 9 of the 12-Step program. Step 9, as we know, is not just about an apology; it requires taking responsibility, "clearing the scorched earth," and demonstrating a true change of attitude on our part. The bot is here to help us do it right.

How can the bot help and guide you?

  • The bot offers step-by-step guidance for all types of amends:

  • 🤝 Direct Amends: You can perform a simulation (role-play) with the bot before the real meeting. The bot will portray the person you harmed, allow you to practice what you want to say, and give you supportive and constructive feedback.

  • 🔄 Indirect Amends (of Goodwill): For cases where direct amends might cause harm. The bot will help you brainstorm alternative actions that prove your change of attitude in practice (such as volunteering or changing habits).

  • 📝 Amends for those you cannot meet: For people with whom contact is in your "black circle." The bot will provide an outline or help you draft an "honest letter" (not actually sent).

  • 🗣️ Processing an Amend already performed: If you have already made an amend, you can share with the bot what happened and receive a safe, inclusive, and non-judgmental space to process the experience.

  • Requirement for Consultation: Any amend you plan to perform must be approved and discussed with your sponsor or a sober fellow member before execution. We do not make amends at the expense of others or cause new harm.
