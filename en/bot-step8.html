The Bridge of Amends: The Journey of Willingness of Step 8

What does this bot do?

The bot offers three work tracks, so everyone can choose where to start based on where they currently are in their recovery.

How can the bot help and guide you?

  • The bot is built with 3 different tracks:

  • 1️⃣ Track 1: Separation of Domains (List vs. Execution)
    A track that focuses solely on building the list and the willingness. It will help you stop when you start thinking ahead and fearing Step 9 (how I will make the amend and what the reaction will be). The bot will bring the focus back only to the facts and to building current willingness.

  • 2️⃣ Track 2: Cultivating Willingness (Working on resistance and resentments)
    Having trouble putting someone on the list because they also harmed you? This track will help you, using the principles of the program, to clear the resentment and see your part independently of the other side. (If it is a deep resentment that is hard to remove, the bot will direct you to do a Step 4 on it first).

  • 3️⃣ Track 3: Analyzing Types of Harms
    A track that requires maximum honesty. It will help you break down relationships and identify hidden harms you might have missed (emotional harms like belittling or manipulation, financial or spiritual harms, or harms to time and trust), and formulate the harm accurately and free of self-flagellation.
