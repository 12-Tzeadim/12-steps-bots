Step 4 - The Reflector. Feedback, Guidance, and Release from Resentment

What does this bot do?

There are quite a few members among us who like to write Step 4 themselves, real "old school" on pen and paper or in a private document. But sometimes, after putting things in writing, there is a need to get some objective feedback and organize one's thoughts before approaching a sponsor for Step 5.


In addition, we all sometimes have old 4th steps – hard and stubborn resentments that gather dust and simply refuse to let go. Here is an excellent opportunity to dust them off and reprocess them again.

What is the bot's role?

You simply share with it a Step 4 you've already written, and it goes over it section by section to help:


Identify the gap between what happened in reality and the interpretation our disease tells us.


See where we let others manage our self-worth.


Honestly pinpoint our exact part (selfishness, self-seeking, dishonesty, and fear) and identify other character defects that might have been hiding there.

How to start?

Simply click the bot link below and send it the following message: "Hi, I want to start working on the fourth step."
