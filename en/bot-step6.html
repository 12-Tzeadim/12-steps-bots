Inventory 6.0 - The Scales

How does the bot help us in social situations?

We all sometimes experience difficulties, friction, or frustration with other people. Step 6 is our tool to identify the character defects that pop up in these situations (such as criticality, a need for control, anger, or people-pleasing), and replace them with spiritual virtues (such as acceptance, consideration, humility, and patience).

What does this bot do?

  • The bot will take you through a process of internal "weighing". It will guide you sensitively, step by step (without overwhelming), and help you examine:

  • ✅ What is the actual payoff you get from that character defect?

  • ✅ What is the loss and the price you pay for it on a daily basis?

  • ✅ How to find the true willingness to let it go, and discover what virtue God would want us to adopt instead.
