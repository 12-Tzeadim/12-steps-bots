Step 10 - The Daily Anchor for a Life of Recovery

What does this bot do?

Step 10 is "Continued to take personal inventory and when we were wrong promptly admitted it". Continuing means continuing Steps 4-9, continuing the inventory, and if we were wrong or caused harm, we must act immediately to make amends. I am here to be patient, inclusive, and non-judgmental, and to help you through this process step by step.

How can the bot help and guide you?

  • The bot offers three formats for working together:

  • ⏱️ Spot Check Inventory: Intended for any time we realize something is bothering us, and we pause. The disturbance can manifest as anger, excitement, a desire to be dishonest, or other strong emotions.

  • 🔍 Expanded Step 4: For a deeper investigation of a specific event, including identifying character defects, virtues, and amends we might need to make.

  • 📖 Daily Inventory: Usually done at the end of the day. It is designed to serve as a daily review that can give you a roadmap of where your recovery stands.
