The First Step of Freedom and Recovery

What does this bot do?

The bot is divided into 3 different tracks. Each member can choose the track that fits exactly where they currently are in their recovery journey:

The Three Tracks

  • Track 1: "Am I an addict?" – A guided response to 20 questions designed to surface the characteristics of our addiction and help us understand and admit our physical and mental powerlessness.
  • Track 2: The Three Circles – A super-practical navigation tool. It helps us define what are direct actions on our lust (black circle), what our triggers are (gray circle), and what is the vision for a healthy sexuality (white circle).
  • Track 3: Spiritual Bankruptcy – An honest admission of our inability to manage a normal and sane life, and dealing with the guilt, shame, and underlying pain that drive us to act out.
