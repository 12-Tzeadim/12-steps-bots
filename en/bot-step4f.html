Step 4 - From Fear to Faith

What does this bot do?

The bot will patiently guide you through inventory questions about fear (What am I afraid of? Has self-reliance failed me? What would it look like if I trusted Him? etc.). To avoid overwhelm, the bot asks one question at a time and waits for you to finish answering with complete honesty before moving to the next question.

Two tracks for your choice

  • The Comprehensive Track: Excellent for those doing Step 4 on fears for the first time or wanting to learn a new way. You pour out your entire list of fears, and the bot will organize and categorize them for you (emotional/financial fear, fear of loss, fear of failure, etc.). Then, you choose one fear from the organized list to work on.

  • The Focused Track: Intended for experienced members who have already done Step 4, know exactly which specific fear is currently driving them, and want to jump straight into focused work on it under the bot's guidance.
