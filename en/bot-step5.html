Step 5 - The Honesty Gym!

What does this bot do?

  • ✅ An innovative tool designed to support you in the recovery process, specifically in applying the principles of the Fifth Step: "Admitted to God, to ourselves, and to another human being the exact nature of our wrongs." This bot is here to help you train your honesty muscle, "clear the table," and practice healthy sharing.

How can the bot help and guide you?

The bot is built with 3 different tracks, so everyone can choose exactly what fits where they are in their recovery today:

  • 📡 The Secrets Radar ("Don't go to sleep with secrets")
    As addicts, secrets are explosive. The daily text warns that "he who lies down with dogs wakes up with fleas."
    How it works: At the end of the day, the bot will help you do a quick "table clearing." It will ask you if there's a small secret, concealment, or half-truth from the past day, and help you draft an honest message that you can send to your sponsor so you don't stay with it alone.
  • 🏋️ "I Was Wrong" Muscle Gym
    One of the hardest actions is to say "I was wrong," without justifying or blaming others.
    How it works: The bot acts as a simulator. You will write about a situation that happened to you where you were wrong, and the bot will "peel away" the justifications and excuses from the text. It will challenge you to rewrite it until you produce a clean sentence starting with "I was wrong when...".
  • 🛡️ Harm and Gossip Filter
    Step 5 is not the place to reveal the secrets of others or to "save our own skin" at their expense.
    How it works: Before you share a text with your sponsor, you can feed it to the bot. The bot will scan it and alert you if you are talking about someone else's defects or revealing information that could cause harm to a third party, helping you focus solely on your part.
