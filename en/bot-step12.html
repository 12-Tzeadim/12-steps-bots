Step 12 - The Spiritual Compass for All Affairs of Our Lives and Carrying the Message

What does this bot do?

This is an AI-based spiritual guide designed to help us process and apply the Twelfth Step.

How can the bot help and guide you?

  • The bot offers 2 primary work tracks. It is highly recommended to complete both tracks from start to finish, each at a time that fits you and your current place in recovery:

  • 🌱 Track 1: "Carrying the Message" (For Sponsors)
    An excellent tool before or after a conversation with a sponsee or a new member. The bot will help you focus your intention, ensure you are sharing from your experience without trying to "fix" or take responsibility for another's recovery, and maintain healthy sponsor-sponsee boundaries.

  • 🔄 Track 2: "Principles in Action" (Daily Application)
    The 12-Step program is not just about recovery from addiction, but a program for empowerment and personal growth. This track will help you take everyday situations (in relationships, at work, with friends) where you struggle, identify which defects or fears are being triggered, and apply the spiritual principles of the program in practice.
